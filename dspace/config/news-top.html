Welcome to TUHH Open Research (TORE)!

TORE is the Research Information System of the Hamburg University of Technology.

TORE contains research data and also the open access repository of TUHH, formerly known as tub.dok. You can find electronic fulltexts here, starting with single articles via the TUHH-journal Spektrum upon to doctoral thesises developed at the TUHH.

Your publication is missing? Members of TUHH can publish scientfic papers created at TUHH on TORE for worldwide and free access. If you have already left TUHH, please register for a local account.

TORE is a service of TUHH library. Ask us about publication services: Contact
