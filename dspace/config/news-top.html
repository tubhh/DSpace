Welcome to tub.dok!

This is the open access repository of TUHH. You can find electronic fulltexts here, starting with single articles via the TUHH-journal Spektrum upon to doctoral thesises developed at the TUHH.

Your publication is missing? Members of TUHH can publish scientfic papers created at TUHH on tub.dok for worldwide and free access. If you have already left TUHH, please register for a local account.
